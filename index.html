How To Play:

  1. Start:

    You are presented with boxes corresponding to the players that match the following prompt:

    Name the ACTIVE players (GK, DEF, MID, FWD) who have scored the most Fantasy Premier League points in a gameweek, including double gameweeks, since the FPL's inaugural season in 2002/03.
    (Player represented by the team they did it with.)

  2. Guess:

    Click the Guess Here button to prompt a screen where you can type and select players.

    Players will be listed by their name and their position.

    To select a player, click the line the player's name and position is on.

  3. Points:
      If you guess/select a player correctly:
    1. Your point total will increase by 1.
      • (Shown by your point total to the left of the "/ 20" .)
    2. The player's name and a checkmark will be revealed on the box that corresponds to that player.
  4. Strikes:
      If you guess/select a player incorrectly:
    1. Your point total will unchanged.
    2. One of the strike indicators in the scoreboard will turn red.
  5. Etc:
      Additional features:
    1. The "Previous Guesses" button shows your previous guesses.
    2. The "Stats" button shows the percentage of people that guess each player.
      • Unlocks after you complete the game.
    3. The "Share" button copies your result (in text format) to your clipboard.
    4. A new trivia is released each night at midnight.

Rules:

  1. Goal:

    Guess the 20 active players (GK, DEF, MID, FWD) who have scored the most Fantasy Premier League points in a gameweek, including double gameweeks, since the FPL's inaugural season in 2002/03.

  2. Game:
      The game ends when:
    1. You get all 20 players correctly.
    2. You get 5 strikes.

    Whichever comes first.

  3. Scoring:
    1. Points:
      • Each correct guess adds one point to your score.
      • The maximum possible score is 20 points.
    2. Strikes:
      • Incorrect guesses count as strikes.
      • Exceeding the allowed strikes ends the game.
      • Repeating a guess will count as neither a strike nor a point.
